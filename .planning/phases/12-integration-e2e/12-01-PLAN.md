**NodeDao.kt** — Change `getPendingChanges` signature to match DocumentDao/BookmarkDao pattern:

Current:
```kotlin
@Query("""
    SELECT * FROM nodes
    WHERE device_id = :deviceId
    AND (content_hlc > :sinceHlc OR note_hlc > :sinceHlc ...)
""")
suspend fun getPendingChanges(sinceHlc: String, deviceId: String): List<NodeEntity>
```

New:
```kotlin
@Query("""
    SELECT * FROM nodes
    WHERE user_id = :userId
    AND device_id = :deviceId
    AND (content_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR note_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR parent_id_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR sort_order_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR completed_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR color_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR collapsed_hlc > :sinceHlc
      OR deleted_hlc > :sinceHlc)
""")
suspend fun getPendingChanges(userId: String, sinceHlc: String, deviceId: String): List<NodeEntity>
```

Note the parameter order: `userId` first, matching DocumentDao and BookmarkDao.
