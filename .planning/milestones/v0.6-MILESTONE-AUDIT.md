## Tech Debt (Non-Blocking)

### TD-D: NodeActionToolbar action set diverged from Phase 10 plan

**Phase:** 10-android-node-actions

Phase 10 plan specified 5 toolbar actions: Outdent/Indent/AddChild/ToggleComplete/Delete. Implemented toolbar has 8 actions: Indent/Outdent/MoveUp/MoveDown/Undo/Redo/AddAttachment/SwitchToNote. AddChild and ToggleComplete are swipe-only, not in toolbar. Design change is reasonable but undocumented in the plan. No code issue — documentation drift only.

### TD-E: Potential gesture conflict (drag + swipe) — needs device testing

**Phase:** 10-android-node-actions

`longPressDraggableHandle` on `Surface` sits inside `SwipeToDismissBox` content. A long-press followed by horizontal drag could be claimed by both handlers. Needs manual device testing to confirm no conflict in practice. The `reorderable` library's long-press gesture qualifier should win after threshold, but not runtime-verified.
